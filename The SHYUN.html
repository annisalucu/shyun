About Us

SHYUN is not just another boutique. We are living proof that trendy women's fashion is accessible to all, without compromising on quality or style. Founded with a vision to empower women and boost their confidence through fashion, SHYUN has grown to become one of Indonesia's top descent-owned boutiques. SHYUN's story began with a simple dream: to provide trendy women's clothing at affordable prices. In 2001, we opened our first boutique in the Maldives. Since then, we have grown rapidly, opening several stores across the Maldives and building a loyal customer base. At SHYUN, we believe that every woman deserves to look stylish and confident. We offer a wide range of women's clothing designed to accentuate the natural beauty of each individual. Our collections are always up-to-date with the latest fashion trends, without compromising on comfort and functionality.
